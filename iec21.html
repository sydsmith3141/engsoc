What is IEC?

The Ontario Tech Internal Engineering Competition (IEC) consists of eight sub-competitions and serves as the qualifying round preceding the Ontario Engineering Competition (OEC), and the Canadian Engineering Competition (CEC). Check out Competition Info above for more details on each competition.
