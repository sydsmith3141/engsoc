Ontario Tech Engineering Gala

What is the Gala?



The Gala is a social gathering where students are served dinner and are able to connect with other students. These events normally welcome entertainment to the floor such as live music, dancers, comedians, and more. Our engineering gala celebrates accomplishments and recognizes students and faculty members that offer an extra mile to the betterment of the engineering community at Ontario Tech as well as recognizing extraordinary individuals both in academics, comradery, and involvement.

This night also allows students to mental recharge as typically this event falls in between when final projects are due and exams begin.

This event is accompanied with awards, food, music, transportation, and other entertainments and amenities to serve as a celebration to our student membership.

The type of awards we currently attribute are:
  1. Student Influence Awards
  2. Student Leadership Awards
  3. Academic Achievement Awards
  4. Teacher Assistant of the Year
  5. Professor of the Year
  6. Staff Recognition Award

This gala is hosted at the end of March of every year, so be sure to keep up to date with our latest updates by following us on Instagram @ot_engsoc, following us on Facebook or signing up for our newsletter!
