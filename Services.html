Services

Your Ontario Tech Engineering Students' Society works hard to provide a wide range of valuable services to you

As a society, Ontario Tech’s Engineering Students’ Society values assisting our engineering students in reaching their individual goals and aims to provide services that accurately represent student needs. In addition to offering academic advocacy, supporting and funding a variety of other clubs and sub-societies, supporting the Faculty of Engineering and Applied Sciences and the school’s Student Union, as a society we also have a mental health initiative called ‘SAFE’ in place for our students.

MHFA

Mental Health First Aid (MHFA) is the support provided to a person who may be experiencing a decline in their mental well-being or a mental health crisis. Ontario Tech Engineering Students' Society is proud to present FREE training for engineering students.

Solidworks Certifications

EngSoc offers two levels of certifications, associate and professional level (CSWA, CSWP).

CSWA

Ontario Tech Engineering Students’ Society will be hosting a SolidWorks workshop for students who are interested in getting their Certified SolidWorks Associate (CSWA) certification. If you are interested in gaining more hands-on experience and knowledge or simply looking to grow your skillsets to become more proficient in SolidWorks then you do not want to miss out on this event! This is an excellent opportunity for you to get ahead and meet industry needs. The certification is authentic and genuine and is provided by Dassault Systèmes - SolidWorks Certification Centre upon successfully passing the examination. Certified SolidWorks Associate (CSWA) is a Level 1 - Intermediate Level workshop.
The format of the workshop is as follows:
Workshop #1: Saturday, October 23rd from 10 AM to 1 PM
Workshop #2: Sunday, October 24th from 10 AM to 1 PM
Workshop #3: Saturday, October 30th from 10 AM to 1 PM
Exam: Saturday, October 31st from 10 AM to 1 PM

CSWP

Due to the high demand and many requests, the Ontario Tech Engineering Students’ Society will be hosting Certified SolidWorks Professional (CSWP) workshops. The certification is authentic and genuine and is provided by Dassault Systèmes - SolidWorks Certification Centre upon successfully passing the examination.
The format of the workshop is as follows:
Workshop #1: Saturday, November 6th from 10 AM to 1 PM
Workshop #2: Saturday, November 7th from 10 AM to 1 PM
Workshop #3: Saturday, November 13th from 10 AM to 1 PM
Workshop #4: Saturday, November 14th from 10 AM to 1 PM
